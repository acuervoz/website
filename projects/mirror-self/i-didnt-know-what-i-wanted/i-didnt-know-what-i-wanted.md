# I didn't know what I wanted

**I didn't know what I wanted to do back then.**

R: So when did you know?

**What?**

R: What you wanted to do..

**I still don't know.**

R: So why did you say you "didn't know" if you still don't know?!

**Because I didn't.**

R: ...

**Sorry, back then *I didn't know*, that is true. And *I don't yet know*. But this is different.**

R: How?

**I think it’s because I’m not the same person I was, that’s why I say I didn’t—**

R: “Know”. Right..

**Now I don’t know something else.**

R: I guess I don’t know either.
